Query parameters, also known as search or query strings, 
    are a part of a URL that provides additional information 
    for the purposes of identifying specific content or operations. 
    They often come after the main URL and start with a question mark (?). 
    They consist of a series of key-value pairs separated by an ampersand (&).
  
        URL: https://www.example.com/page
        Query Parameters: parameter1=value1&
        parameter2=value2
        Key: parameter1, Value: value1
        Key: parameter2, Value: value2
